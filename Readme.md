MFX AI Loader

MFX AI Loader is a WPF Windows application that lets you load GGUF AI models and chat with them. It includes basic conversation management for keeping track of your chats.

Features

Load any GGUF AI model from your computer

Chat interactively with the AI

Keep track of conversation history

Requirements

Windows 10 or later

.NET 6.0 or higher

GGUF-compatible AI models
